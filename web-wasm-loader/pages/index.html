FAQs:

Camera controls?

You can use the arrows or WASD or ZQSD or your mouse or your touch screen

What are the white strips on the circuit?

The checkpoints used to reward the cars, +1 point per checkpoint.

What are the green lines on the leading car?

The car's sensors, the "eye" of the car, it hit the ground and the "walls".

What is the white line on the leading car?

The car's "ground sensor", it help reward the car when passing checkpoints.

What are the white/grey lines alongside the circuit?

The skelton used to build the circuit, interpolated with a BSpline.


Technologies

  • it is develloped in C++ (using the emscripten compiler to target WebAssembly).
  • it uses Bullet Physics for the physic.
  • it uses OpenGL-ES3/WebGL2 for the rendering.
  • it was built on a Linux OS.

Note

The 'success' of this simulation is quite random, which mean that sometime you might want to reload this page and hope for a better result.
